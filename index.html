Research

My research interests revolve around looking at the consequences of the strong coupling of quantum systems to their environment and especially on its impact on energy transport. This problematic emerges from biological systems (e.g. photosynthesis in plants) where strong coupling seems to be central for explaining observed phenomenons. More generally I work on Open Quantum Systems and especially Tensor Networks techniques. The working title of my thesis is `A Quantum Theory of Biological Systems'.
