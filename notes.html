Open Quantum Systems |Œ®>

Documentation and notes on several aspects of Open Quantum Systems. Some of those notes have been made with Jupyter notebook.

Open Quantum Systems correspond to sub-parts of larger closed systems. The part we are interested in is generally called the system and the remaining part is called the environment. Generally the physicist task is to get the dynamics of the system without solving the exact dynamics of the environment.

The study of Open Quantum Systems is important as all objects around us are not isolated. If atom where not interacting with their (electromagnetic) environment, there would never be electronic de-excitation and thus no spontaneous emmission.
